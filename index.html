Overview

The Workshop on Election Science and Technology (WEST'20) aims to disseminate the best new ideas in election science theory and practice, with an emphasis on empiricism, scientific rigor, and peer review. We value ideas from all sectors of the election world, from technologists to social scientists to election practitioners. The inaugural workshop will be colocated with the Election Verification Network Conference.
